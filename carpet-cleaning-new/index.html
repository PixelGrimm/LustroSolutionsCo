Why Choose Professional Carpet Cleaning?

Regular vacuuming only removes surface dirt. Over time, carpets accumulate deep-seated dirt, dust mites, pet dander, allergens, and stains that regular cleaning cannot remove. Professional carpet cleaning not only restores your carpet's appearance but also improves indoor air quality and extends the life of your carpets.

At Lustro Solutions Co., we use professional-grade steam cleaning equipment and eco-friendly cleaning solutions to deep clean your carpets, removing up to 95% of dirt and allergens. Our methods are safe for your family, pets, and the environment while delivering exceptional results.

Our Professional Carpet Cleaning Process

We follow a systematic 7-step process to ensure your carpets are thoroughly cleaned:

1. Pre-Inspection

We assess your carpet's condition, identify problem areas, stains, and determine the best cleaning method for your specific carpet type.

2. Pre-Vacuuming

Using commercial-grade vacuums, we remove all loose dirt, dust, and debris from the surface and deep within the carpet fibers.

3. Pre-Treatment

We apply eco-friendly pre-treatment solutions to high-traffic areas and stains, allowing them to break down before deep cleaning.

4. Hot Water Extraction

Our professional steam cleaning equipment injects hot water and cleaning solution deep into carpet fibers, then extracts it along with dirt, stains, and allergens.

5. Stain Removal

We use specialized stain removal treatments for stubborn marks, including pet stains, wine, coffee, ink, and other common household stains.

6. Deodorizing & Sanitizing

We apply eco-friendly deodorizers to eliminate odors and sanitizers to kill bacteria and allergens, leaving your carpets fresh and healthy.

7. Grooming & Drying

We groom the carpet pile to restore its appearance and apply quick-dry treatments. Most carpets are dry within 4-6 hours.
