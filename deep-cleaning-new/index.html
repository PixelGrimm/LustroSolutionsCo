What is Deep Cleaning?

Deep cleaning goes far beyond regular maintenance cleaning. While regular cleaning focuses on surface-level tidiness, deep cleaning involves a comprehensive, thorough cleaning of every area in your property, including those hard-to-reach places that are often overlooked during routine cleaning.

Our professional deep cleaning service in London is designed to eliminate built-up dirt, grime, bacteria, and allergens that accumulate over time. We use professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly cleaning products to ensure your property is not just clean, but sanitized and healthy.

Our Deep Cleaning Process

At Lustro Solutions Co., we follow a systematic, room-by-room approach to ensure no area is missed:

1. Pre-Cleaning Assessment

We conduct a thorough walkthrough to identify all areas requiring attention, assess the level of cleaning needed, and create a detailed cleaning plan tailored to your property.

2. Top-to-Bottom Cleaning

We work systematically from top to bottom, starting with ceilings, light fixtures, and high surfaces, working our way down to floors. This ensures that any dust or debris falls onto areas we haven't cleaned yet.

3. Deep Sanitization

Using eco-friendly, professional-grade cleaning products, we sanitize all surfaces, eliminating bacteria, viruses, and allergens. We pay special attention to high-touch areas like door handles, light switches, and remote controls.

4. Hard-to-Reach Areas

We clean behind and under furniture, inside appliances, and in all those corners and crevices that are often overlooked. Nothing is too difficult for our experienced team.

5. Final Inspection

Before we finish, we perform a comprehensive quality check to ensure every item on our cleaning checklist has been completed to the highest standard. If anything needs attention, we address it immediately.
