Our Professional Window Cleaning Process

We follow a systematic approach to ensure streak-free, spotless windows:

1. Preparation

We protect surrounding areas with drop cloths and prepare our professional cleaning solutions and equipment.

2. Pre-Cleaning

We remove dust, cobwebs, and loose debris from window frames, sills, and tracks before cleaning the glass.

3. Glass Cleaning

Using professional squeegees and eco-friendly cleaning solutions, we clean all glass surfaces using a systematic top-to-bottom, left-to-right technique to prevent streaks.

4. Frame & Sill Cleaning

We clean and wipe down all window frames, sills, and tracks to remove dirt, dust, and water marks.

5. Final Inspection

We perform a final quality check to ensure all windows are streak-free and spotless. Any areas needing attention are immediately addressed.
